About Shubh Shop

Shubh Shop is a simple e‑commerce demo site made using HTML, Tailwind CSS, and ES6 JavaScript. It demonstrates how to use localStorage for saving users, products, and cart data. Explore the Men, Women, and Kids sections to simulate a full shopping experience.
